ABOUT

When we think about interactive entertainment, we commonly tend towards triple A games on the latest consoles, or live-streaming platforms like YouTube or Twitch as the premiere embodiments of interactive entertainment.

We aim to REDEFINE the limits of what interactive entertainment can be, by introducing a way for individuals to enact real-time physical changes through a few simple clicks on a website, while receiving real-time feedback on those very changes.

Hence, we hope to be the initial spark that IGNITES a new wave of interactive entertainment based on interfacing software-hardware at a user-friendly level, in order to provide the highest levels of entertainment for the MASSES.
